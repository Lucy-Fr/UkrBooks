Yevhenia Kuznietsova

Biography

Yevheniia Kuznetsova (born 30 June 1987 in Kryvyi Rih) is a Ukrainian writer, translator, and media researcher...

She has worked as a journalist, taught postcolonial studies, and served as a consultant...

Kuznetsova debuted with the humorous culinary book "Cooking in Sorrow" (2020)...

As a translator, Kuznetsova works with both fiction and non-fiction...

Kuznetsova notes that Ukrainian has always been her native language...

She lives between Ukraine and Spain, raises two children...
